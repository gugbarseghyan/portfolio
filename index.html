About

I’m Goog, a graphic and UI designer focused on creating clear, meaningful, and emotionally driven visual experiences. My work sits at the intersection of design, storytelling, and psychology, where visuals are not only seen, but felt.

I work across branding, UI design, social media advertising, crowdfunding, and product presentation, helping ideas become structured, consistent, and human. I approach each project by understanding the deeper vision behind it the message, the emotion, and the experience a brand wants to create.

Alongside my design work, I’m writing a science-fiction book that explores identity, self-discovery, and the connection between the human mind and the universe. This process strongly influences how I design: I think in narratives, systems, and worlds, not just layouts and colors. Every project becomes a story with its own logic, rhythm, and meaning.

I value simplicity, strong concepts, and details that quietly elevate the experience. I’ve completed my military service and I’m currently open to collaborations, freelance work, and long-term creative partnerships.
